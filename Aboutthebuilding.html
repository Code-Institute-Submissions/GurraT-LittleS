Vision

We want to offer an oasis in the middle of the city pulse where you can feel at home. The neighbourhood is the perfect match if you wants to have near to the city, shopping and restaurants and at the same time find a comfortable environment where you can feel relaxed after an intense day. Your comfort is our pleasure.

History

1650 - The Island is mentoined in the Dorpat as resort where youth sailed to and played in the nature

1786 - The fort Nya Chartagho was inducted at the island

1824 - The Island was sold to a private person

1851 - The schooner "Sweden" was build on the Island - which won a silver medal in Americas Cup

1908 - AB Lux (later renamed to Electrolux) is installed at the Island.

1935 - Edmund Sparmann constructed Airplanes for the military at the Island

1936 - A bridges between the Main land (Kungsholmen) and Island is constructed

1990- Electrolux is leaving the Island and living houses is built

Today- The Island serve mainly as a living area, close to the water, the city and with a variety of eating-out alternatives
