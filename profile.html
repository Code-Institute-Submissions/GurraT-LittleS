Checklist for Moving In

Pickup your keys before 12 pm at our office, keep in mind to bring your ID



Avoid blocking elevator or parking in the middle of the street while moving in



Locate your storage space which is marked with your Apartment ID and install a locker



Go over your Inspection protocol, if you find any deficiencies make sure to report them within 8 days notice



familiarize yourself with the neighbourhood and find the parking and storage room for bicycle in the basement



Located the table for booking washing time in the attic outside the laundary room, your lock is marked with your Aprtment ID


Get acquainted with the neighbours, if you planning a moving in party make sure to set up a notice in the entrance so you neighbour are aware



Checklist for Moving Out

Notify the tax authorities about your new adress



Fix remarks on inspection protcol before moving out



Clean your apartment throughfully



Sort and depose your waste



Return all keys to apartment, laundary and storage
