General Information

Our tenants are our priority. Our aim is to offer apartments where you can feel at home. In our building you can find everything you need for the everyday life. Of course you have access to laundry and storage room. Our building is located close to the city in a quiet neigbourhood. On the island you find everything from supermarket to bakery. The local bus leaves frequently and it is easy to park in the neighbourhood. We beleive our strenght is that we care for our costumer.

You are most welcome to Contact us or Report an Issue . We priorities your call will do our best to solve your issue.
