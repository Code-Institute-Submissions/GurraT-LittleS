Checklist for Moving In

Pickup your keys before 12 pm at our office, keep in mind to bring your ID



Avoid blocking elevator or parking in the middle of the street while moving in



Locate your storage space which is marked with your Apartment ID and install a locker



Go over your Inspection protocol, if you find any deficiencies make sure to report them within 8 days notice
