Guidelines

When should i turn-off the turn off the music ??

We recommend that you turn-off the music after 22 pm, regarding the sound environment see Jordabalken § 3:1

I am allowed to have pets in the Apartment ??

You are allowed to have pets in the apartment but make sure to prevent damage on the apartment and provide an adequate environment for your pets, for more information see Lag (2007:1150) om tillsyn över hundar och katter

What should i think of when it comes to Fire regulation ??

All apartements are equiped with Smoke detectors, make sure to not block stairways and common spaces with items, for more information see Boverkets overview of fireregulations

Is smoking allowed within the building ??

As stated within your contract it is not allowed to smoke inside the apartment, for more information see Jordabalken 12 kap 24§

Is it allowed to make barbeque on the balcony ??

For fire safety reason it is not allowed to make barbeque on the balcony

Where do I through my Garbage ??

A Garbage room for ordinary waste is found in the basement, for more detailed sorting of garbage such as plastic, papper and metals a collection point is found outside Essingbrogata 37

Where can I store the items i do not use ??

Storageroom is found in the attic, be aware of fire regulations and make sure not to occupy common space in the attic

Does the building have a common Laundary ??

Laundary is found in the attic, table for booking washing time is found outside the laundary room, everyone got their own key to specific lock on the booking table

Is Water included in the rent ??

Cold and Hot water is included in the rent

Is Electricity included in the rent ??

Electricity is not included in the rent, see link to compare electricity company

Is gas for the owen included in the rent ??

Gas is not included in the rent, see link for more information regarding gas suppliers

Where do I find Information about Television/Broadband/Telephony ??

Television/Broadband/Telephony is not included in the rent, see link for more information regarding Television/Broadband/Telephony

What happens if I loose my key ??

When moving in you will receive 3 keys to your apartment, 2 keys to the booking table outside the laundary room and two keys to the attic and basement, lost keys will be charge and withdrawn from the next invoice

Is my apartment insured by default ??

he tenants are responsible to arrange with their own Home insurance, see link for more information regarding Home insurances

Where can I store the items i do not use ??

Storageroom is found in the attic, be aware of fire regulations and make sure not to occupy common space in the attic
